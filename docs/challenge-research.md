# Challenge research register

AmplifyHub challenge copy must be original. External material is used to identify common skills, scenarios, and evidence—not copied as scripts.

| Source | Used for | Review cadence |
|---|---|---|
| Existing 40-lesson AmplifyHub curriculum | Core exercises, prerequisites, terminology | Every curriculum release |
| Gong Labs objection and discovery research | Objection buckets, opening, discovery, CTA scenarios | Quarterly |
| HubSpot sales role-play and mock-call guides | Timed drills and scenario formats | Quarterly |
| Salesforce Trailhead cold-calling module | Entry-level prospecting task coverage | Quarterly |
| Cognism State of Cold Calling | Current objection patterns | Quarterly |
| Beginner SDR and manager interviews | Relevance, clarity, missing practice needs | Five interviews per quarter |
| Challenge telemetry and feedback | Completion, time accuracy, skips, helpfulness | Monthly after pilot |

## Editorial checklist

- One observable skill and one primary action.
- Safe for a career changer without an employer or paid sales tool.
- Clear call context: cold call, discovery call, follow-up, offline preparation, or optional real-world practice.
- No fabricated claims, aggressive pressure, prospect PII, contact details, or recordings.
- Includes measurable objectives, estimated time, difficulty, and an original example.
- Retire or revise after 20 assignments when completion is below 25%, helpfulness below 60%, or “not relevant” is the leading skip reason.
