The "not interested" drill

You're ten seconds into a cold call with Dana, who owns a small auto-detailing studio. Before you finish your first sentence, Dana cuts in:

D
Dana · prospect

"Thanks, but we're not interested."

What do you say next?

The pattern to learn: acknowledge the brush-off → redirect with one easy, specific question. Ten seconds in, "not interested" is a reflex — not a decision.
